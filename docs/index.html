Glacier

Glacier is the step-by-step protocol for storing bitcoins in a highly secure way

Highly secure

The Glacier protocol was designed to account for all sorts of exploits and hacks that anyone could use to get their hands on your Bitcoin wallet.

Self-managed and easy

Glacier was designed to be easy to follow so that you can secure your wallet without needing any third parties or intemediaries. Security should be accessible to everyone, not just the tech elite.

Open source

Glacier is open source so that everyone in the community can use, extend it, and improve it. If you spot any weaknesses or vulnerabilities you're welcome to contribute.
